THE STRETCHY PRICING ENGINE — Final Mechanic



This is the heart of the platform. Keep it this simple everywhere it's explained.



















How it works



The host sets two numbers. That's it.











1. Their financial target — what they need to earn from the session (covering venue, costs, and their own time).











2. Their minimum spots — the smallest number of people needed for the session to be worth running.











The platform does the rest.











(Target + $20 + GST) ÷ minimum spots = starting price











The starting price is the most anyone will ever pay. It's anchored to industry standard — because the maths works out that way when hosts set a realistic target and a fair minimum. A yoga host targeting $200 with 8 minimum spots starts at ~$28. That's the going rate for casual yoga in Auckland. Fair from the start.











From there, every new person who joins drops the price for everyone. The room fills, the price falls, the session gets better. That's Stretchy.



















The four phases





Phase 1 — HOLD



Session is open. Attendees hold a spot. No charge. Free to cancel.Price shown = starting price (the most they'll pay).Progress bar shows: "X of [minimum] spots held."Price doesn't drop yet — only confirmed spots count.





Phase 2 — 36-HOUR CHECK



36 hours before the session:











Minimum met → session CONFIRMED. All holders locked in. No more cancellations.



Minimum not met → session CANCELLED. All holds released. No one charged. Transparent comms sent to all.











Edge case: if a session is posted with less than 36hrs notice (last-minute pop-up etc.), the system skips to the 2hr lock-in. Bookings open until 2hrs before, then price locks and payment processes.





Phase 3 — LIVE DROP



Session is confirmed and happening. New people can still join — up to venue capacity.Every new joiner drops the price for everyone already holding.Existing holders get notified as price drops.No cancellations. You're in.











Scarcity trigger (80% full): When 80% of spots are taken, the session card shows "⚡ Filling fast" and existing holders get: "[Session] is nearly full — share it to drop the price further and grab those last spots."(Maximum venue capacity lives in the backend. It's never shown as a number until the 80% trigger fires.)





Phase 4 — LOCK-IN



2 hours before the session:Price freezes. Everyone — original holders and late joiners — pays the same final price. Stripe charges. Receipt sent.



















The host pricing prompt



When a host creates a session, after selecting session type and duration, the platform surfaces a quiet sanity check:











"For a 60-min casual vinyasa in Auckland, fair market range is $25–35. At your $200 target with 8 minimum spots, your starting price is $28. Feels about right."











For one-off events or longer sessions, it adjusts:











"For a 2-hour breathwork workshop, typical rates sit between $65–95. At your $400 target with 6 minimum spots, you'd start at $77."











This is a reference, not a rule. The host always has the final say — and the platform makes that clear.











Override: Hosts can adjust their target freely at any point. Special event, exclusive venue, longer format, charity session — the prompt is a starting point, not a gate. The override is always one tap away, no friction, no explanation required.

ABOUT PAGE











A social movement.



Stretchy is an IRL movement community for people who want to move — without the membership, the markup, or the loneliness of doing it alone.











We connect people through vetted, local sessions. Yoga, run clubs, breathwork, sound baths, pilates, dance. Led by people who know their practice and their community. Priced to get better the more people show up.











Here's the thing: movement is better together. And fairer together, too. The more people who join a Stretchy session, the less everyone pays. That's not a gimmick — it's the whole point. Community is the engine.



















Why Stretchy exists.



Fitness got expensive. Classes got lonely. And the gap between a fair price and feeling like you belong somewhere kept getting wider.











We started in Tāmaki Makaurau (Auckland) with a simple idea: what if the price of a yoga class dropped as more people joined? What if your neighbours, your workmates, the person you always see at the park were all moving together — and all paying less because of it?











That's Stretchy. A community that literally gets better — and cheaper — the more people are in it.



















Why join.



Because movement is better when it's shared.











Because $28 is already fair for a casual class — and it only gets better from there.











Because the Social Stretch after (coffee, tea, a drink, a chat) is sometimes the best part.











Because walking into a room of people who all chose to show up, move, and connect — that's not a gym class. That's community. And community makes everything feel lighter.



















Stretchy in Auckland — Stretchy Yoga IRL.



Stretchy started with yoga. In Auckland. In community halls, local parks, and borrowed spaces. Before the platform, before the app, there were just people who wanted to move — and a host with a mat and a belief that movement should be accessible to everyone.











Stretchy Yoga IRL was the first expression of what we're building. Intimate, neighbourhood-based sessions led by local teachers. It's still running. It's still the heartbeat of this community.











The platform is how we bring that feeling to more people, more places. The feeling of walking into a room and knowing you're all there for the same reason. Movement. Connection. A good stretch.











Auckland is where we started. Other cities are coming. Join the waitlist and we'll bring Stretchy to you.

HOW TO STRETCHY — UI COPY



Yellow card copy — appears on: Place Held (04) · Home scroll (01) · Onboarding · Session Detail (03)







1. A session is listed. Price starts at a max. There's a minimum number of people needed to make it happen.



2. Hold your place. No charge yet. Once enough people hold, the session is on — and the price starts dropping. Tell a mate. Tell a workmate. Tell a random.



3. 36 hours out — go or no go. Minimum met? Session confirmed. The more people who join from here, the lower everyone's price. No cancellations from this point.



4. Two hours out — doors close. Final price locks. Everyone pays. That's it.



5. Show up. Move. Social stretch. ✌️
